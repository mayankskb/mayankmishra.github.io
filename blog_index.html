Convolutional Neural Networks, Explained

March 7th 2019

ConvNets are inspired by the biological vision system where every neuron responds to stimuli only in the restricted region of the visual field called a receptive field. In other words, when we saw an image each neuron works in its own receptive field. These neurons are connected in a way as to cover the entire visual field.
Convolution Network works on the same ideology. Every neuron process data for its receptive field only. The layers are arranged in a way to detect simpler patterns first (lines, curves, etc) and further identifying more and more complex patterns (faces, objects, etc).
